Is this another "side hustle quiz" gimmick?
No. The quiz takes ~3 minutes, the output is a 90-day playbook with daily moves, and you get an AI coach that's read it. The quiz is the cheapest part — the playbook is where the work is.
What if I already have a side project?
Tell the quiz. It'll re-frame the playbook around your current product instead of recommending a new one. People use HustleAI more for "I'm 4 months in, stuck, what now" than for cold-start matching.
How is this different from ChatGPT + Notion?
ChatGPT doesn't remember your weighted match. It doesn't sequence 90 daily moves. It doesn't nudge you on day 47 because day 39's milestone slipped. You can build this stack yourself — most people don't, which is the whole point.
Pricing?
Match is free. Playbook + AI operator is a flat monthly under what a single coaching call costs. No annual lock-in.
What if my hustle is wild / niche / outside your training data?
The model doesn't pick from a fixed menu. It synthesises from your inputs. If your match is "fermented hot sauce for esports teams" — the playbook will be for that.
